About me

I am a native Portuguese speaker and translator from English to Portuguese and Spanish to Portuguese.

I have an academic background in translation as I completed a three-year Degree in Applied Languages at the Faculdade de Letras of Universidade do Porto, Portugal.

I began my career as a professional translator at JABA Translations, where I worked as an in-house translator and later, also as the Assistant Chief of the English Department.

There, I have enhanced my experience on technical translation, proofreading and CAT tools, especially SDL Trados, memoQ, SDLX and SDL Passolo.

I also had the chance to naturally develop my decision-making skills, as I had to manage and negotiate the projects’ deadlines with the Project Managers.

After working in this company, I've started a professional internship in another translation agency (Local-IT, connected to Philos Comunicação) with somewhat similar tasks, but different working methods.

The more targeted subjects for translation were IT, software/hardware, mobile apps and games, telecommunications, tourism as well as transcription tasks.

My main fields of expertise are electronic devices, software, the automotive industry and the mechanical field, but I also took part in several projects related to videogame localization, tourism, marketing, press, and e-commerce, as well as a major e-learning project, more creativity-oriented.

What am i up to now?

Currently, after 10 years of experience as an in-house translator, I've decided to "take the leap" and provide my linguistic services solely as a freelance translator.

My goals

I seek to help companies meet their linguistic needs from English and Spanish into Portuguese through an open, clear, honest and devoted collaboration.

I'm ready for your project!

It is a pleasure helping to connect people!
